What are The Types of Car Insurance?

Our car insurance options offer tailored coverage at competitive rates. Enjoy exceptional customer support when you need it and peace of mind on every journey.

Comprehensive Car Insurance

Comprehensive Car Insurance offers the widest coverage for your car.
It safeguards your vehicle against many risks, including damage from accidents,
natural disasters like storms or floods, as well as theft, hijacking and loss.
It also includes third-party coverage.


Third-Party, Fire & Theft Car Insurance

Third-Party, Fire and Theft Car Insurance covers damage caused to third parties,
like other vehicles or property, in an accident where you're at fault. Additionally,
it protects against loss or damage to your vehicle due to fire or theft. Unlike
comprehensive insurance, it doesn't cover other types of damage to your own vehicle,
such as car accidents. This option offers a balance between cost and coverage as it
protects against significant risks like theft and fire.


Third-Party, Fire & Theft Car Insurance

If your car is valued at under R250,000, TM Car Insurance Lite cover
could be right for you.TM Car Insurance Lite offers three affordable
options for vehicles in the lower price bracket. TM Car Insurance Lite 1
provides cover for stolen or hijacked vehicles. You will be covered for
up to R1 million in third-party cover. TM Car Insurance Lite 2 insures your car
if it’s written off after an accident, covers up to R1 million for third-party damage,
and theft and hail damage cover. The Accident Assist benefit, which includes towing
and storing your car after an accident, is included. TM Car Insurance Lite 3 covers
your car if it’s written off after an accident, up to R1 million third-party cover,
theft and hail damage insurance, and limited accident cover. Accident Assist is included.


Motor Warranty

Don’t get caught by unexpected repair costs. Our Motor Warranty offers protection
beyond the manufacturer's warranty period. It covers repairing or replacing
components that fail due to mechanical or electrical failure. Keep your car
running smoothly and avoid breakdown and repair costs. We offer two vehicle warranty
options: Auto Plan and Auto Drive Warranty. If your vehicle is under three years old
and has done less than 120,000km, Auto Plan could be the right option for you,
while Auto Drive is recommended for vehicles under seven years old and under 250,000km.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is there such a thing as cheap car insurance?
    • Yes, with TM Car Insurance you can get cheap car insurance. In fact, we have one of SA’s lowest insurance quotes. We believe insurance isn’t just for expensive cars – everyone deserves to have some level of vehicle cover. Whether you need Comprehensive Car Cover or a TM Car insurance Lite policy, we’ll make sure you have cover that fits your budget.
  • Can I use a vehicle repairer of my choice?
    • You can only use a TM Car Insurance-approved repairer to fix your car after an accident. We’ve found some of the best repair professionals at the most affordable prices. All repairs come with a lifetime guarantee. We manage the claims process from start to finish to ensure you get the best service.
  • How do I lower my car insurance premiums?
    • Drive safely, as the longer you go without an accident, the lower your risk becomes. Installing an approved tracking device can also reduce premiums since it improves chances of recovery if your car is stolen.
  • Which insurance cover is best for a car?
    • There isn’t a single ‘best’ cover. We offer six levels of insurance to fit different needs. The best insurance for you depends on your car’s value, age, and mileage. Talk with a TM Car Insurance consultant to find the right coverage for you.
  • Will my premiums decrease as my car gets older?
    • No, because insurance premiums aren’t solely based on your car’s value. Most claims are for repairs, and repair costs rise over time. This keeps insurance premiums stable or increasing, even as your car loses value.
